The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Possesing the great sense of smell and superior traking instincts, the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports as foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.

Trivia

  1. Height - 13-16 in (33-41 cm)
  2. Weight - 22-25 lb (10.0-11.3 kg)
  3. Coat -Short haired, hard coat of medium length
  4. Colour -Tricolor or white in combination with black and tan/brown or brown/tan
  5. Life Span - 12-15 years
